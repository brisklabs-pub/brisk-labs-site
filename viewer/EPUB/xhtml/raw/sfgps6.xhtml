Assignment 3.  Writing Sentence with Uncommon Words with Context Clues

Look for unfamiliar word in the dictionary or any reading material.  

Write 5 sentences of that unfamiliar word with different types of context clues. 

For example: 

Uncommon Word:  Pulchritude

Meaning:  Beauty

 

Definition 

Pulchritude is a characteristic to describe a beautiful person.  

Antonym 

Unlike the ugliness of her evil stepsister, she is blessed with pulchritude.

Synonym 

She is the epitome of pulchritude, beauty, and loveliness.  

Examples 

Being good with people, and taking care of herself from inside and out is the essence of pulchritude.  

Explanation 

It is pulchritude when she shows herself in a very attractive and appealing way with her looks and the way she treats people well.   

 

Write them on a piece of paper with your name on it and submit it to your teacher.

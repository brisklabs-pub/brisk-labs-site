Assignment 1.  Using Synonyms and Antonyms

 

Read your favorite reading materials, magazines, books, or any reading materials that you love to read.  

Get at least 5 words which is unfamiliar or difficult.  

Look for the meaning of the word, its synonyms or antonyms.  

Use them into sentence.  1 for synonyms.  1 for antonyms.

Write them on a piece of paper with your name on it and submit it to your teacher.
